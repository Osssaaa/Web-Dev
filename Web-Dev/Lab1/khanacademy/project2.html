Fruit Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Banana 1
Strawberries 1/2 cup
Milk 1 cup
Honey 1 tsp

Steps:

1. Put all fruits into blender.

2. Add milk and honey.

3. Blend until smooth.
